Cat Sitter Guide

Thank you for taking care of Gray Cat and Mr. Tabs!
Here's everything you need to keep them happy, fed, and loved.

🚨 In case of emergency: Please reach out to me directly using the contact info you already have on hand. Don't hesitate — I always want to know!

Daily Feeding Schedule

GRAY CAT & MR. TABS
Morning
Breakfast — 6:00 – 7:00 AM
  • The dry food dispenser will automatically release food into their bowls — no action needed.
  • They'll graze on this throughout the day, so no need to empty the bowls.
  • Check the dry food canister level — if it's empty, refill it.
    • Refill dry food is in the metal bin on the floor across from the laundry machine.
Evening
Dinner — 7:00 – 8:00 PM
  • Add one can of wet food to each gray bowl (separate from the automatic dispenser bowls).
    • Wet food is in the box near the dry food dispenser.
  • They won't finish it all at once — they graze throughout the evening, so leave the bowls out overnight and rinse them the next day.
Water
Fresh water daily
  • Refill the green water bowl when it's looking low.
  • Tap water is fine, but the Brita in the fridge works great, too.

Litter Robot

  • I'll text you if anything needs to be done with the litter — we get alerts when litter is low or the drawer needs to be changed
  • Extra litter is in the crate next to the Litter Robot — use it to refill when levels are low

Gray Cat Tips

  • He has a lot of energy — don't be alarmed by big jumps!
  • He loves to cuddle once he settles down
  • He may try to climb things — this is normal and fine, but please make sure he does not chew the TV corners.
😌

Mr. Tabs Tips

  • He is very calm and will likely want cuddles
  • His loud purring is normal, he's just happy!
  • Slow blinking back at him makes him very happy
🚨

In Case of Emergency

If anything seems off — a cat isn't eating, acting strangely, is injured, or you're just unsure — please reach out to me right away using the contact info you have. No issue is too small to flag. I would always rather know! Thank you so much for looking after these two. They are very loved. 🐾

General Notes

Both cats are indoor only — please make sure all doors and windows are closed when coming and going.

They are best friends and keep each other company, so don't worry if they're napping together in a pile.

Treats are fair game! Feel free to give them some if they're being extra sweet — or if you need to lure them out of hiding. The Temptations yellow bag is in the cabinet above the fridge. 🐾
